## Why Use the Average Deal Size Calculator?
Helps sales managers understand the value of their average client.

### Formula
$$ \text{Average Deal Size} = \frac{\text{Total Revenue}}{\text{Number of Deals}} $$

## How-To Guide

## FAQs
### What is Average Deal Size?
It is the average revenue generated per closed-won deal.
### How is it calculated?
Divide the total revenue by the number of deals.
### Why does it matter?
It helps in forecasting and setting sales targets.
### Can it fluctuate?
Yes, it often varies by season or marketing push.
### How to increase average deal size?
Through upselling, cross-selling, and targeting enterprise clients.
### Is this free?
Yes.
