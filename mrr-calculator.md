## Why Use the Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) Calculator?

Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) is the core operational metric of any subscription business. It measures predictable subscription revenue flowing into your SaaS business every month.

Having accurate, real-time insights into this metric helps SaaS founders, CFOs, and growth teams optimize capital allocation, protect operating runway, and achieve top-tier venture scalability.

## Mathematical Formula & Derivation

The mathematical derivation for this financial metric is expressed as:

$$\text{Total MRR} = (\text{Starter Count} \times \text{Price}) + (\text{Pro Count} \times \text{Price}) + (\text{Enterprise Count} \times \text{Price})$$ \n$$\text{Blended ARPU} = \frac{\text{Total MRR}}{\text{Total Subscribers}}$$ 

Where all input values are evaluated over a standardized monthly or annual accounting period.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)?
MRR is the total predictable subscription revenue generated by active customers in a single calendar month.

### What should NOT be included in MRR?
One-time setup fees, professional services, consulting revenue, and hardware sales should be excluded from MRR.

### How does annual billing convert to MRR?
Annual prepaid contracts are divided by 12 to calculate their monthly recurring revenue contribution.

### Is subscriber data stored on a server?
No. All calculations run strictly inside your browser.
