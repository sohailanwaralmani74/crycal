## Why Use the Sales Quota Attainment Calculator?
It gives you a clear picture of how close you are to your goals.

### Formula
$$ \text{Attainment} = \left( \frac{\text{Achieved Sales}}{\text{Sales Quota}} \right) \times 100 $$

## Real-world Comparison
| Scenario | Attainment Rate |
|---|---|
| Underperforming | < 80% |
| On Target | 90% - 110% |
| Overachieving | > 110% |

## How-To Guide
1. Enter your **Achieved Sales**.
2. Enter your **Sales Quota**.
3. View the **Quota Attainment (%)**.
