## Why Use the Pipeline Coverage Ratio Calculator?
It helps sales leaders forecast if they have enough potential deals to reach their sales goals.

### Formula
$$ \text{Coverage Ratio} = \frac{\text{Pipeline Value}}{\text{Sales Target}} $$

## Real-world Comparison
| Ratio | Status |
|---|---|
| < 2x | High Risk |
| 3x - 4x | Healthy |
| > 5x | Excellent |

## How-To Guide
1. Enter the **Pipeline Value**.
2. Enter your **Sales Target**.
3. Get your **Coverage Ratio**.

## FAQs
### What is a Pipeline Coverage Ratio?
It compares your open pipeline to your sales target.
### What is a healthy ratio?
Most experts recommend a 3x or 4x pipeline coverage ratio.
### How do I use the calculator?
Input your pipeline value and target.
### Why is 1x not enough?
Because not all deals in the pipeline will close.
### How to improve the ratio?
Generate more leads and opportunities.
