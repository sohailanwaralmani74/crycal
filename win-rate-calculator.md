## Why Use the Win Rate Calculator?
Understanding your win rate helps identify areas for improvement in your sales process.

### Formula
$$ \text{Win Rate} = \left( \frac{\text{Won Deals}}{\text{Total Opportunities}} \right) \times 100 $$

## Real-world Comparison
| Industry | Average Win Rate |
|---|---|
| SaaS | 20% - 25% |
| Agency | 30% - 40% |
| Real Estate | 15% - 20% |

## FAQs
### What is Win Rate?
Win rate is the percentage of total sales opportunities that result in a closed-won deal.
### How do you calculate Win Rate?
Divide the number of won deals by the total number of opportunities and multiply by 100.
### What is a good Win Rate?
It varies by industry, but typically 20% to 30% is considered standard.
### How can I improve my Win Rate?
By qualifying leads better and improving the sales pitch.
### Should I include lost deals?
Yes, total opportunities includes both won and lost deals.
